ABOUT

AAKRITI is an initiative to applaud the Technical creativity of students across the country. Being the first of its type, It will surely emerge as the biggest online technical fest of India. It provides platform to showcase the talent of young aspiring minds. In its first Iteration as AAKRITI’20, the team has combined certain most anticipated technical events associated with computer science and related emerging technologies.
Apart from this we are also trying to collaborate with incubation firms to incubate the ideas of young Enterpreneurs and provide a platform as well as technical and financial support to promising start-ups.
